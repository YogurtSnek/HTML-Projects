Mori Calliope



Mori Calliope is a respectable figure in the VTubing space with over 2 million subscribers to her name in Youtube.
The main reason why I choose to be one of her subscribers is her music, a lot of it is considered by some cringey or of bad taste, but I believe there is genuine emotion to be found in the songs she sings in.

I wish to now share 3 of my favorite songs and why I enjoy them:

  • Off with their heads: I put much value into the lyrics of a song and this one delivers confidence straight into me, enough to tackle the day and whatever else may come. The following piece of art was made for the song, depicting Mori Calliope in a beautifully adorned kimono.
  • Holy嫉妬: Another great one for boosting my mood and making me feel great, but unlike the last one, this one implements the rap in a fast and snappy fashion. Sure, it may not have as much flare as the last one but it is a song I always am compelled to sing, no matter what. The japanese characters in the song's name are pronounced like "Shitto", so the songs name in a strange way is called Holy Shitto. The following artwork was made for her album, Shinigami Note, where this song was released.
  • CRINGECORE: While the past songs may have had flare or amazing vocals, this one speaks to the tiny little bit of self-loathing inside of oneself, enough to help me overcome some of it while giving amazing lyrics that make you bop your head up and down at every opportunity.
    The following artwork was made for her album, Sinderella, where this song was released.
